Assign the person to a group based on their age.

Ages 1-2:   Infant
Ages 3-4:   Toddler
Ages 5-11:  Child
Age  12:    Preteen
Ages 13-18: Teenager
Ages 19-25:	Young Adult
Ages 26-55: Adult
Ages 56+:   Senior

Notes:
Should validate input provided is a positive number, return ArgumentException if it is 0 or below.
Should only expect a whole number

Examples:
Given:
	2
Return:
	Infant

Given:
	23
Return:
	Young Adult
